Last updated 02.12.2023

1. How to delete your data

1.1 Backup Data

After uploading 3 backups your first backup is automatically deleted. If you want to delete all of your backups, please contact us using the contact form.

1.3 Leaderboard Data

If you have registered to the leaderboard you can delete your entry/data anytime by clicking on the trash symbol on the leaderboard page.
